REGARDING TO THE REGISTRATION PROCESS


  • Step 1: Click on the register icon on our website.
  • Step 2: Enter your personal details(name, age, sex, phone number, address).
  • Step 3: Enter your skills in which you are specialized.
  • Step 4: LABOR card must be compulsory, enter your labour card number.
  • Step 5: Enter the type of work you needed.
    • Daily basis
    • 5days a week
    • For a week
    • For a month
    • Yearly access
  • Step 6: Cross verify before the submission.
  • Step 7: Then click on submit to save your responses.


AFTER THE SUBMISSION OF THE REGISTRATION FORM.


  • After the process of submission of the form we will display your responses to the owners.
  • Once owner get satisfied by your skills he will book the worker one he/she is needed.
  • Then we will notify you.
  • You will receive a conformation message that your work will be confirmed for tomorrow.
